Knowledge Center

Krishi Tips & Government Schemes

Stay informed about best farming practices, weather advisories, and schemes that put money back in your pocket.

☀️
PM-KUSUM Solar Pump Scheme
Get up to 90% subsidy on solar irrigation pumps under PM-KUSUM Yojana. Apply online or through your nearest Krishi Kendra.
🌧️
Fasal Bima – Crop Insurance
Protect your crops against drought, flood and unseasonal rain. Premium as low as 2% for Kharif crops under PMFBY.
🌡️
Weather Advisory – Vidarbha
Pre-monsoon rains expected in Nagpur & Amravati by 15 June. Prepare field bunding and sow cotton after first rain.
🏦
Kisan Credit Card (KCC)
Get up to ₹3 lakh credit at 4% interest for buying seeds, fertilizers and other inputs. Apply at any nationalized bank.
🧑‍🌾
Soil Health Card
Know your soil NPK, pH and micronutrients. Free testing at Krishi Vigyan Kendras. Use results to optimize fertilizer use.
📱
eNAM – Online Mandi
Sell directly on the National Agricultural Market platform. Get best prices from buyers across India without leaving your village.
